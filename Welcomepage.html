ABOUT ME

Health monitoring System

The project's goal is to create an easier method to monitor and track patients health via the internet.

Team 5



The purpose of this project is to have isolated positive Covid-19 patients’ being able to be updated with their families at the hospital without being exposed to the virus about their current health data while being treated.

The potential market for the project was for hospitals who have to isolate their patients due to Covid-19 infections. The family of said isolated patient(s)’ will be able to have a live health data on the patient. Now the new potential Market has expanded for all types of patients in the hospital or at home-care where our equipment is very convenient for the patient to move around without restriction to medical hardware that have to be attached to the patient.
